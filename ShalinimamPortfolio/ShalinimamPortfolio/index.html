Who am I?

I'm Dr.Shalini Mahato

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Computer Engineering at the National Institute of Advanced Manufacturing Technology (NIAMT), Ranchi, Jharkhand, India (formerly NIFFT). I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Birla Institute of Technology, Mesra, Jharkhand, India in 2021. Prior to that, I earned my M. Tech degree in Computer Systems and Technology in 2013 and my B. Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering in 2008 from Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University (formerly WBUT), West Bengal, India.

Experience

Before entering academia, I worked as a developer at Tata Consultancy Services (TCS). Over the course of my career, I have contributed to more than 20 research papers, with 7 of them indexed in SCI and 6 indexed in Scopus. I also have a project selected in the MSME Idea Hackathon 2022, which is sponsored by the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME) Innovative Scheme, Government of India. I am proud to have been the winner of the MSME Idea Hackathon 2022.

Research Experience

In addition, I have filed 3 Indian patents. I co-convened the International Conference on “Global Mental Health and Public Health Challenges and Innovation (GMHPHCI’22)” organized by the Indian Institute of Information Technology (IIIT), Ranchi, Jharkhand, India. I have also served as a resource person for several Faculty Development Programs (FDPs), seminars, and conferences. As a reviewer, I have contributed to journals such as IEEE Access, Journal of Medical Systems, Journal of Intelligent & Fuzzy Systems, and others.
